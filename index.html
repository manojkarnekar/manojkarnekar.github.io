About Me

With a Master's in Physics from IIT Bombay and over 2 years of industrial robotics experience, I specialize in developing autonomous systems that solve real-world challenges. My journey spans from theoretical physics research in string theory to deploying autonomous robots internationally.

At Flo Mobility, I've led groundbreaking projects including AI-powered road anomaly detection systems for Nissan deployed in Japan, autonomous construction robots with 600kg payload capacity in Dubai, and precision lawnmowers for TVS. My expertise extends across the full robotics stack - from sensor fusion and SLAM to cloud integration and fleet management.

I'm passionate about bridging academic rigor with industry innovation, currently serving as a Board Member for Physics Curriculum Development at University of Mumbai, where I integrate modern robotics and AI trends into academic frameworks.
